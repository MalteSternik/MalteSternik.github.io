about me

I am a PhD researcher at Ruhr-Universität Bochum working at the intersection of computational linguistics and historical language change. My research focuses on how meaning evolves in language over time, combining distributional semantics with corpus-driven methods. I am particularly interested in how computational models can capture subtle linguistic phenomena that traditionally required human expertise to detect.
